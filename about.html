About

I am a front-end developer and upcoming back-end developer from Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Inspired by my father, I begun learning about the ropes of computers and slowly the QBasic language. Then, I started making my first basic website about US Presidents that started my progression using HTML and CSS.

I taught myself HTML, CSS, and Responsive web design even more with online tutorials and classes in college. In addition, I learned more programming languages to improve the functionality of my work, and my future clients. I enjoy designing and developing ideas into websites that can be viewed by family or new customers.

Whenever I am not making websites or doing anything related to code, I produce house music on the side using Ableton.

I believe that we are in an era where people want quick, responsive websites. My style of work is minimal with very little colors.
